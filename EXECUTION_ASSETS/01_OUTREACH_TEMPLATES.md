## Batch Sending Tips

### LinkedIn (10-15 per day)

**Morning Batch** (9-10am):
- Send 5-7 connection requests with note
- Personalize each one
- Mention mutual connection or how you found them

**Afternoon Batch** (2-3pm):
- Send 5-7 direct messages to connections
- Follow up on pending requests

**Don't**:
- Send more than 15/day (looks spammy)
- Use exactly same message (LinkedIn flags)
- Send to people with no e-commerce connection

### Email (20-30 per day)

**Tools**:
- **Mail merge**: Use Gmail + Sheets (free)
- **Mailchimp**: Free up to 500 contacts
- **Gmass**: $19.95/month

**Best practices**:
- Personalize first line (store name, detail)
- Keep under 150 words
- One clear call-to-action
- Send Tuesday-Thursday 9am-11am
