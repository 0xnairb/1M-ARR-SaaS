## Market Scoring Framework

### The 4-Dimensional Market Evaluation

Score each potential market on these 4 dimensions (1-10 scale):

#### 1. Pain Severity (How much do they hurt?)

**Score: _____/10**

**Questions to Answer**:
- Is this a "hair on fire" problem or a "nice to have"?
- Are people actively searching for solutions RIGHT NOW?
- What's the cost of NOT solving this problem?
- How frequently does this pain occur?

**Scoring Guide**:
- **10/10**: Critical problem, business breaks without solution (e.g., payment processing, security)
- **8-9/10**: Major pain, significant time/money loss (e.g., compliance, workflow automation)
- **6-7/10**: Moderate pain, clear inefficiency (e.g., reporting, collaboration)
- **4-5/10**: Minor pain, occasional frustration (e.g., nice-to-have tools)
- **1-3/10**: Barely a problem (don't build)

**Evidence Needed**:
- [ ] Found 20+ people complaining about this online in last 30 days
- [ ] People are DIY-ing solutions (proof of pain)
- [ ] Existing tools in this space (proof of demand)
- [ ] Can quantify the cost (time or money)

#### 2. Economic Value (How much is it worth?)

**Score: _____/10**

**Questions to Answer**:
- Does solving this save money or make money?
- Can you quantify the ROI in dollars?
- Is the value 10x+ your intended price?
- Do they have budget allocated for this?

**Scoring Guide**:
- **10/10**: Direct revenue impact, easily 100x ROI (e.g., sales tools, revenue optimization)
- **8-9/10**: Clear cost savings, 20-50x ROI (e.g., automation, efficiency)
- **6-7/10**: Indirect value, 10-20x ROI (e.g., productivity, quality)
- **4-5/10**: Soft benefits, hard to quantify (e.g., happiness, convenience)
- **1-3/10**: No clear economic value (don't build)

**ROI Calculation**:

**Evidence Needed**:

- [ ] Customers can expense/budget this category
- [ ] ROI payback in <90 days
- [ ] Value increases over time (not one-time)

#### 3. Market Size (How many potential customers?)

**Score: _____/10**

**Questions to Answer**:
- Are there 10,000+ potential customers?
- Is the market growing or shrinking?
- Can you access them through identifiable channels?
- Is there room for a new player?

**Scoring Guide**:
- **10/10**: Massive market, 100K+ potential customers, rapid growth (e.g., e-commerce, SaaS)
- **8-9/10**: Large market, 50-100K potential customers, steady growth (e.g., specific industries)
- **6-7/10**: Medium market, 10-50K potential customers (e.g., niche verticals)
- **4-5/10**: Small market, 1-10K potential customers (e.g., micro-niches)
- **1-3/10**: Tiny market, <1K potential customers (too small)

**Market Size Estimation**:
```
Total Addressable Market (TAM): _______
Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM): _______
Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM): _______

Example:
TAM: All dentists in US = 200,000
SAM: Private practice owners = 120,000
SOM: Private practices with 3+ dentists = 40,000
```

**Evidence Needed**:
- [ ] Can list 100 potential customers right now
- [ ] Industry reports show market size
- [ ] Clear distribution channels exist
- [ ] Market is growing >5% annually

#### 4. Ability to Win (Can you dominate this?)

**Score: _____/10**

**Questions to Answer**:
- Do you have unique insight or unfair advantage?
- Can you build an MVP in 4-8 weeks?
- Do you have access to early customers?
- Can you compete with existing players?

**Scoring Guide**:
- **10/10**: Expert in this space, insider access, unique tech advantage (unfair advantage)
- **8-9/10**: Deep knowledge, network in space, clear differentiation
- **6-7/10**: Some experience, can learn quickly, viable positioning
- **4-5/10**: Outsider, steep learning curve, commodity positioning
- **1-3/10**: No advantage, can't compete (don't build)

**Unfair Advantages**:
- [ ] Domain expertise (worked in this industry)
- [ ] Network (know 50+ potential customers)
- [ ] Technical advantage (proprietary tech/data)
- [ ] Distribution advantage (existing audience)
- [ ] Speed advantage (can ship 3x faster)

## Go/No-Go Decision Framework

### Final Decision Checklist

**Before committing to build, verify ALL of these**:

#### Must-Haves (All must be YES)

- [ ] **Pain Score >7/10**: This is a real, severe problem
- [ ] **Value Score >7/10**: Clear economic value, quantifiable ROI
- [ ] **Market Score >6/10**: Large enough market (10K+ potential customers)
- [ ] **Ability Score >6/10**: You have some advantage or can build one
- [ ] **Total Score >7/10**: Overall strong opportunity
- [ ] **15+ Problem Interviews**: Talked to real potential customers
- [ ] **Can List 100 Customers**: Know where to find them
- [ ] **Willing to Pay**: 30%+ of interviews showed willingness to pay
- [ ] **Can Build MVP in <8 Weeks**: Technically feasible
- [ ] **Excited About This Space**: Would happily work on this for 2+ years

#### Nice-to-Haves (More = Better)

- [ ] **Network Access**: Know 10+ potential customers personally
- [ ] **Domain Expertise**: Worked in this industry before
- [ ] **Growing Market**: >10% annual growth
- [ ] **Weak Competition**: Clear gaps in existing solutions
- [ ] **Clear Distribution**: Know exactly how to reach customers
- [ ] **Recurring Revenue Model**: Natural subscription fit
- [ ] **Low Churn Potential**: Sticky product, high switching costs

#### Red Flags (Any = Pause & Reconsider)

- [ ] **Crowded Market**: 20+ well-funded competitors
- [ ] **Declining Market**: Shrinking industry
- [ ] **Long Sales Cycles**: >6 months to close
- [ ] **Regulatory Blockers**: Legal/compliance barriers
- [ ] **Can't Reach Customers**: No clear distribution channel
- [ ] **One-Time Revenue**: Hard to make recurring
- [ ] **Not Excited**: Don't care about this problem
- [ ] **Can't Quantify Value**: Soft benefits only

## Market Idea Brainstorming

### 10 Questions to Find Your Market

1. **What industry did you work in?** _________________
   - Can you solve an industry-specific problem?

2. **What frustrates you weekly?** _________________
   - Your pain = others' pain?

3. **What do you spend money on?** _________________
   - If you pay for it, others will too?

4. **What manual process takes you hours?** _________________
   - Can you automate it?

5. **What data do you wish you had?** _________________
   - Can you aggregate it?

6. **What tools don't work well together?** _________________
   - Can you integrate them?

7. **What regulation is painful to comply with?** _________________
   - Can you simplify it?

8. **What are your friends/network complaining about?** _________________
   - Can you solve it?

9. **What's changing in your industry?** _________________
   - What new problems does it create?

10. **What do you know that others don't?** _________________
    - Can you productize that knowledge?

### Market Idea Validation Pipeline

**Stage 1: Brainstorm (1 hour)**
- List 10-20 market ideas
- Don't filter yet, just brainstorm

**Stage 2: Quick Score (2 hours)**
- Score each on Pain × Value × Size × Ability
- Eliminate bottom 50%

**Stage 3: Research Top 3 (6 hours)**
- Deep research on top 3 markets
- Complete research templates

**Stage 4: Customer Interviews (2 weeks)**
- 15-20 interviews for each top market
- Document learnings

**Stage 5: Final Selection (1 day)**
- Complete go/no-go checklist
- Make decision
- Commit to one market

**Total Time Investment**: ~40 hours spread over 3 weeks

**ROI**: Prevents 6-12 months of building wrong thing
