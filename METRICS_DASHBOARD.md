### 6. REVENUE RETENTION & EXPANSION

#### Net Revenue Retention (NRR)

| Metric | This Month | Last Month | Target |
|--------|------------|------------|--------|
| **MRR Start of Month** | $_______ | $_______ | - |
| **Expansion MRR** | $_______ | $_______ | - |
| **Churned MRR** | $_______ | $_______ | - |
| **Net Revenue Retention** | _____% | _____% | >100% |

**Formula**:
```
NRR = ((MRR at start + Expansion MRR - Churned MRR) / MRR at start) Ã— 100

Example:
MRR Start = $50,000
Expansion = $3,000
Churned = $2,000

NRR = (($50,000 + $3,000 - $2,000) / $50,000) Ã— 100
NRR = ($51,000 / $50,000) Ã— 100
NRR = 102%
```

**What it means**:
- **>100%**: Expansion revenue offsets churn (growth from existing customers)
- **90-100%**: Healthy retention, some expansion
- **<90%**: Churn problem or lack of expansion opportunity

## Metric Benchmarks by Stage

### Pre-$10K MRR

**Focus Metrics**:
- Activation rate (>40%)
- Time to value (<3 days)
- Product engagement

**Don't Worry About**:
- Perfect churn (still finding PMF)
- LTV:CAC (sample size too small)
- Growth rate (too early)

### $10K-$100K MRR

**Focus Metrics**:
- MRR growth (>15%/month)
- Churn (<10%/month)
- Trial â†’ Paid conversion (>15%)
- CAC by channel

**Targets**:
- LTV:CAC >3:1
- Payback <12 months
- 90-day retention >60%

### $100K-$1M ARR

**Focus Metrics**:
- NRR (>100%)
- Churn (<5%/month)
- CAC efficiency
- Sales efficiency

**Targets**:
- LTV:CAC >5:1
- Payback <6 months
- 90-day retention >70%
- Gross margin >75%
