- **Complex**: Extensive documentation and ongoing monitoring

- Manual processes overwhelm small teams

- Enables enterprise revenue (high value)
- Clear ROI calculation

**Market Size**: 7/10
- TAM: ~100,000 small SaaS companies globally

- Growing market (+26% CAGR)

**Ability to Win**: 5/10
- ‚ùå High technical complexity (security expertise required)

- ‚ùå Requires deep compliance knowledge

## Risk Analysis

### Key Risks

**1. Platform Risk**
- **Risk**: Shopify or Stripe changes API, pricing, or policies
- **Mitigation**: Multi-platform strategy, direct relationship with platforms
- **Likelihood**: Low (APIs are stable)

**2. Competitive Risk**
- **Risk**: A2X or new entrant drops prices or adds features
- **Mitigation**: Focus on superior UX and customer success
- **Likelihood**: Medium

**3. Market Risk**
- **Risk**: E-commerce slowdown
- **Mitigation**: Reconciliation needed regardless of growth
- **Likelihood**: Low (structural trend toward e-commerce)

**4. Technical Risk**
- **Risk**: Reconciliation accuracy issues
- **Mitigation**: Extensive testing, gradual rollout, human review option
- **Likelihood**: Medium (complex problem)

**5. Churn Risk**
- **Risk**: Customers churn after setup
- **Mitigation**: Ongoing value (daily syncs), analytics features, sticky integrations
- **Likelihood**: Medium (need to prove ongoing value)

### Mitigation Strategies

1. **Multi-platform from Year 1** (not just Shopify)
2. **Direct API partnerships** (reduce platform risk)
3. **Focus on retention** (customer success, ongoing features)
4. **Build community** (users help users)
5. **Expand value over time** (analytics, forecasting, tax prep)
